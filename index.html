About us

Lost Senses was created by IB students to foster empathy and understanding for the blind and visually impaired community.

Through our unique "blind friendship dates" experiences, participants get a glimpse into the daily challenges faced by those without sight.

2.2 Billion Visually impaired people worldwide
43.3 Million Are completely blind
